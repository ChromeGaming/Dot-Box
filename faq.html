Frequently Asked Questions

**Creating an Account:** 1. **Visit the game's official website** and look for a 'Sign Up' or 'Register' option. 2. **Fill in the required information**, such as your email address, a username, and a password. Make sure to use an email address that you have permanent access to. 3. **Verify your email address** by clicking on a verification link sent to your email. 4. **Complete any additional steps** required by the website, which may include setting up security questions or verifying your age.
